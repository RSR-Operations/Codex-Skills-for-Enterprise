# Meeting Intelligence Output Patterns

## Executive Recap

Use for senior leaders who need the minimum viable context.

```text
Executive Summary
- ...

Decisions
- ...

Risks / Escalations
- ...

Actions
- ...
```

## Action Register

Use when the meeting outcome needs to enter a tracker.

| Owner | Action | Due date | Dependency | Status | Source note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Not stated | ... | Not stated | ... | New | ... |

## Follow-Up Message

Use a short, clear message:

```text
Subject: Follow-up: <meeting topic>

Team,

Here are the confirmed decisions, actions, and open questions from <meeting>.

...
```
