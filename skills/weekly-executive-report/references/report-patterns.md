# Weekly Executive Report Patterns

## Board-Facing Weekly Snapshot

Use when the report is for investors, board observers, or executive committee members.

- Lead with business impact and material risk.
- Avoid internal process detail unless it affects forecast, timeline, or customer trust.
- Include decisions needed only when executive action is required.

## Operating Review

Use when leaders need a management rhythm artifact.

| Priority | Status | Progress | Risk | Owner | Next commitment |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ... | Green / Yellow / Red | ... | ... | ... | ... |

## Tone

Prefer crisp, declarative language:

- "Launched pilot with 3 enterprise teams; adoption signals are positive but support load is higher than expected."
- "Timeline is at risk because legal review is not scheduled."
